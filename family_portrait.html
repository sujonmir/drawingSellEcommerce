Family Portrait

Sort By

Here at Charlie’s Drawings we believe every family should be able to have a beautifully hand drawn portrait which is why we offer stunning artwork at such affordable prices.

From just £15 per person we will create a stunning family portrait that you will treasure forever! We can draw any size family, no matter how big or small, great grandmas to tiny babies and even our furry friends can all be drawn together to make a truly special family portrait.

What’s more, we draw straight from your photos and can even draw family members together from separate photos and make it look as though everyone is together!

We also offer additional prints and canvases of your family portrait so for a small additional fee, you can have duplicate copies of your masterpiece to give to the whole family as gifts! A family portrait is a one of a kind personal gift that is sure to be loved and appreciated by everyone, even fussy Uncle Ernie!

Ordering could not be easier, all you have to do is upload your photos via our website, select the amount of people and the size you would like for your portrait and then checkout! Once your drawing is complete, we will send you an email copy of it so you can approve it, once you’re happy with it, we will send it straight to you so your family portrait is ready to take pride of place on your wall!

All we need to draw your beautiful family portrait is good quality clear photos. These don’t need to be professional quality by any means, even clear photos taken on your phone will be perfect!

Charlie’s Drawings has now been running for over three years! We have an amazing team of extremely talented artists and an excellent customer service team. This is why we have so many customers that keep coming back to us and love every portrait drawing as much as their first!

All you have to do to see evidence of this is look on our Facebook page, we have hundreds of visitor posts leaving us such lovely reviews and we appreciate every single one! Feel free to head over to our Facebook and Instagram pages, you will see fantastic customer reviews, lots of examples of family portrait s and other work, sometimes we release special deals and discount codes exclusively for fans of our pages and we even do competitions and giveaways so its well worth joining our 240,000 fans and giving us a like and a follow!

We don’t just do family portraits, we can do anything from a wedding portrait, drawings of your favourite holiday snapshots and anything else you can come up with! We have done some fantastic themed pictures in the past, if you have any ideas, feel free to drop us a message and we’ll see what we can do!
